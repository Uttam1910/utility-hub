Online Notepad

Jot down your ideas or quick notes. Your notes are saved in your browser until you clear them.

How to Use the Online Notepad

  1. Type your notes in the text area provided.
  2. Click "Save" to store your notes in the browser's memory.
  3. Click "Copy" to copy your notes to the clipboard.
  4. Click "Clear" to erase your current notes.
